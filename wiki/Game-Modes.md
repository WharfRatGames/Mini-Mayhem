# Game Modes

## Hotseat
Two players share one device, passing it back and forth each turn. No account required. Supports 2–4 teams with CPU opponents (Easy / Medium / Hard).

## VS CPU
Solo play against one or more AI-controlled teams. Good for learning the weapons and terrain without an opponent.

## Take a Turn (TAT)
Async online multiplayer — each player takes their turn whenever they have time, like chess by mail. Matches are stored server-side and can span hours or days. Requires an account.

- Turns expire after **14 days** (opponent is awarded the win on timeout)
- Supports **Casual** and **Ranked** (ELO-rated) queues
- Scrap is awarded on match completion

## Live Game
Real-time online 1v1. Both players connect simultaneously to the dedicated server, which runs the full simulation authoritatively. Requires an account.

- Supports **Casual** and **Ranked** queues
- **Ranked** matches affect ELO and contribute to leaderboard stats
- If a player disconnects, a **3-minute reconnect window** opens — match resumes when they return, or the remaining player wins by forfeit
- Scrap is awarded on match completion

## Test Mode
Developer sandbox unlocked from the title screen. Gives every team access to all weapons with infinite ammo and sets the turn counter to 100 (unlocks all time-gated weapons). Map seed is displayed on screen.
