# Crate Drops

Each turn has a **30% chance** of dropping a crate somewhere on the map. Crates fall from the sky and land on the terrain surface. Walking into a crate picks it up automatically.

---

## Crate Types

| Type | Chance | Contents |
|---|---|---|
| **Weapon** (brown) | 75% | One weapon from the weapon pool |
| **Health** (white/red) | 25% | +25 HP to the collecting soldier |
| **Scrap** (teal) | +15% bonus\* | 5–30 scrap currency |

\* Scrap crates are a separate 15% roll on top of the main crate drop — multiplayer matches only.

---

## Weapon Pool — Rarity Tiers

| Rarity | Weapons | Chance each |
|---|---|---|
| **Common** | Mine, Shotgun, TNT, Grapple, Baseball Bat, Plasma Torch | ~10% |
| **Uncommon** | Blasthive, Meteor Bomb, Sacred Ordnance, Air Strike | ~8% |
| **Rare** | Black Hole, Revolver | ~3% |
| **Ultra Rare** | Hand of Jerry | ~2% |

## Notes

- Crates are **destructible** — a direct explosion will destroy a crate before anyone can pick it up
- In **Hotseat / VS CPU**, scrap crates do not appear (multiplayer only)
- The crate drop position is random but always placed where there is terrain to land on (not over open water)
