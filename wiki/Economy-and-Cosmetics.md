# Economy & Cosmetics

---

## Currencies

### Scrap (🔩)
Soft currency earned through multiplayer matches. Cannot be purchased directly.

| Source | Amount |
|---|---|
| Win a match | +75 |
| Lose a match | +25 |
| Scrap crate pickup | +5 to +30 |
| Daily login | +25 |
| 7-day login streak | +150 bonus |
| Daily challenges | +30 to +60 |
| Weekly challenges | +150 to +250 |

Scrap is **not awarded in single-player** (Hotseat / VS CPU).

### Warbonds (💎)
Premium currency. Obtained through purchases — not earnable in-game. Used for exclusive cosmetics not available for scrap.

## Challenges

Challenges auto-track progress during multiplayer matches. Claim rewards manually from the challenges screen.

### Daily
| Challenge | Goal | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| Play a match | 1 match | +30 🔩 |

| Get kills | 3 kills | +45 🔩 |
