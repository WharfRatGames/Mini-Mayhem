# Soldiers & Customization

Each team has **4 soldiers**. You can name them and customize their appearance individually from the **MY TEAMS → ROSTERS** menu.

---

## Per-Soldier Customization

| Slot | Options |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Up to 20 characters |
| **Hat** | 12 styles (1 requires scrap/warbonds, see [Economy](Economy-and-Cosmetics)) |
| **Uniform color** | 7 colors |
| **Boot color** | 5 colors |
| **Gun style** | 7 styles (visual only) |

Cosmetics are visible to your opponent in live multiplayer — they're synced through the server every tick.

---

## Team Color

Each team is assigned a color (Red, Blue, Green, or Yellow) that tints the soldier's helmet and team indicator. Colors are chosen in the lobby before a live match starts.

---

## Headstones

When a soldier dies, their headstone is placed at the death location. The headstone style is set per-team (all 4 soldiers in a team share the same headstone). All base headstone styles are free.

---

## Avatar

Your account has a single avatar icon displayed in the lobby and on the leaderboard. Avatars are set in the account settings.

---

## Soldier Stats

Each soldier starts with **100 HP**. Damage is dealt by explosions based on distance from the blast center — the closer to the center, the more damage.

| Condition | Effect |
|---|---|
| HP reaches 0 | Soldier dies, headstone placed |
| Fall damage | Taken when landing after a long drop or knockback |
| Drown | Instant death if knocked into the water |

A team is eliminated when all 4 of its soldiers are dead. The last team standing wins.
